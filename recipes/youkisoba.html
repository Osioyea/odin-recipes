Youkisoba

You's "niceoodles"

These noodles topped with an ommelet can bring a smile to anyone's face jut by looking at them.

This it the shack's signature item and the only one that anybody wants so satisfaction is guaranteed.

Ingredients

  • Yakisoba noodles
  • Sliced pork belly
  • Carrot
  • Green Onion
  • Onion
  • Cabbage
  • Yakisoba Sauce
  • Eggs
  • Salt & pepper
  • Ketchup

Instructions

  1. Cut you meat & veggies: chop green onion & cabbage, julienne the carrot, and slice the onion.
  2. On medium-high heat, brown meat in pan. Gret ready to stir-fry ALL the things.
  3. Add onion - cook until wilted.
  4. Add the rest of your veggies and until soft.
  5. Lower heat to medium & add noddles and keep stirring.
  6. Add the sauce, as much as you like!
  7. Move the noodles onto a plate.
  8. Beat eggs, water, salt and pepper in a small bowl until blended.
  9. Pour mixture into a seperate pan on medium-high heat.
  10. Gently push cooked portons from the edge to the center to let uncooked egg reach the pan.
  11. When no visible liquid egg remains genly place finished omelette over the noddles.
  12. Draw whatever you would like on the omelette with ketchup.
  13. Enjoy!
