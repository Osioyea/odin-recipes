STEWSHINE

Mari's SHINY stew

Stewshine is a culinary achievement made from special ingredients from around the world.

A single bowl costs about $750 to make but don't worry about the price, the taste is well worth it.

Ingredients

  • Lobster
  • Abalone
  • Sazae
  • Marbled beef
  • Sunfish
  • Red snapper
  • Bear paw
  • Snow crab
  • Caviar
  • Bamboo shoots
  • Sashimi
  • Matsutake mushrooms

Instructions

  1. Throw everyting into a big pot.
  2. Mix well.
  3. Done and ready to serve!
